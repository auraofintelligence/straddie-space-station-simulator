After The Tetris Comes The Town

The layout simulator creates fixed geometry. The next layer is behavioural: guest flows, rail robots, AI concierge decisions, queues, water, waste, power and wellbeing across seasons.

01

Sports Team Weekend

Twenty to eighty minimalist guests arrive in waves, shower at similar times, store gear, sleep, eat off-site and leave a tight turnover window.

02

Backpacker Secure Mode

Travellers use lockers heavily, need late check-in, want privacy, charge devices, ask AI concierge questions and move through shared wet modules all day.

03

Podcast And Civic Media Day

Recording capsules, compute pods and check-in modules become a tiny media station for local stories, grant material, civic simulations and public updates.

04

Disaster Support Mode

Sleep capsules become rest infrastructure, AI concierge routes information, compute modules run local summaries, and water/waste limits become first-class constraints.

05

Robot Maintenance Night

Cleaning robots, pod movers, waste movers and inspection crawlers run after low-noise hours while AI schedules rail conflicts and closed modules.
